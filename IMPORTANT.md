### Disclaimer & Development Information

This localization fix is based exclusively on the pre-existing official translation resources extracted directly from the game/mod files. The developers bear no responsibility for any missing, altered, or undocumented strings that remain untranslated.

* **Gathering IDs:** You can manually collect any missing string IDs and submit them to us.
* **Forking the Repository:** You are welcome to fork this repository to implement your own corrections.

All valid community changes and collected data will be integrated into future updates for the Interslavic localization.
